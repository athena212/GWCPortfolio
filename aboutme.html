Scratch: Angry Birds Escape

Here is the scratch game I made with Rhee. This is a combination of angry birds (shooting) and panda pop. The goal of the game is to "rescue" as many birds as possible. If the bird shot is the same type of bird as the bird hit, the bird hit can fly away. This was accomplished by hiding the hit bird if the costume of the shot bird is the same as the costume of the hit bird.
